What is Rollover?

Sunrun Flex program allows you to "accumulate" unused energy (below baseline) for future months.

Low-usage month

(Below baseline)

(For future use)

What They DON'T Tell You

Myth:

"With rollover, you won't pay extra if you exceed baseline in some months."

Reality:

Rollover only smooths out bills but DOES NOT eliminate Flex charges when you exceed your total annual baseline.

Important:

Accumulated kWh are not redeemable for cash and expire at the end of the contract period.
